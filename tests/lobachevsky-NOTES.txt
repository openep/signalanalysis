SOURCE: https://physionet.org/content/ludb/1.0.1/
DOI: https://doi.org/10.13026/eegm-h675

The files contained within `lobachesky` comes from the Lobachevsky University
Electrocardiography Database, from PhysioNet, available at the above URL; the
README and LICENSE for the complete database are included here. The files
included here are a subset from the complete database to allow testing and
evaluation of the functions in this library.

The sample rate for these data are 500 Hz.
