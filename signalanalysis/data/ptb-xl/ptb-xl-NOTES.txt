SOURCE: https://physionet.org/content/ptb-xl/1.0.1/
DOI: https://doi.org/10.13026/x4td-x982

The files contained within `ptb-xl` comes from the "PTB-XL, a large publicly
available electrocardiography dataset", from PhysioNet, available at the above
URL; the LICENSE for the complete database is included here. The files included
here are a subset from the complete database to allow testing and evaluation of
the functions in this library.

The sample rate for these data are 500 Hz.
